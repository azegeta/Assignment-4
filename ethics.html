IT-Related Ethics

Guidelines for Developers

The ACM Code of Ethics guides software engineers to contribute to society, avoid harm, and maintain honesty. For more details, visit the ACM Code of Ethics.

Ethical Considerations for Data Scientists

Data scientists should uphold transparency, minimize bias, and ensure data privacy. These values are highlighted by the Data Science Ethics Project.

As data becomes more integral to decision-making, the ethical responsibilities of those who handle it grow. Professionals must ensure that automated systems do not reinforce societal biases and must advocate for data practices that prioritize fairness, accountability, and transparency.

Sources: ACM.org, DataScienceEthics.com
